HOW TO USE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Step 1: Start the App
   npm start
   (scan QR code with Expo Go)

Step 2: Wait for Model to Load
   You'll see: "Loading hand detection model..."
   (this takes about 2 seconds)

Step 3: Position Your Hand
   • You'll see a YELLOW SQUARE in the center of the camera
   • Move your hand into that square area
   • The square should clearly show where to place your hand

Step 4: Signal Your Gesture
   • When your hand is positioned in the square
   • Tap the BLUE BUTTON at the bottom-right that says "Tap Here to Signal"
   • This signals that you're ready to make a gesture

Step 5: Make Your Gesture
   • After you tap, the app waits for you to make a gesture
   • Status message says: "Hand detected! Make a gesture..."
   • Make any of the 9 recognized gestures

Step 6: See Result
   • After 1 second, a gesture is detected
   • The detected gesture appears in the results panel below
   • Status updates to show which gesture was detected
   • Gesture added to "Recent Gestures" history

Step 7: Repeat
   • Gesture disappears after 3 seconds
   • Button becomes active again
   • You can tap again to signal the next gesture

TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

📍 POSITIONING:
   ✓ Place your hand inside the yellow square
   ✓ Keep hand fully visible in the square
   ✓ Don't overlap the edges

🎯 GESTURING:
   ✓ After tapping button, wait 1 second
   ✓ Then make a clear, distinct gesture
   ✓ Hold the gesture for 1-2 seconds
   ✓ Keep hand in the detection zone

💡 LIGHTING:
   ✓ Good lighting helps detection
   ✓ Avoid harsh shadows on your hand
   ✓ Natural light or bright indoor lighting is best

⏱️ TIMING:
   ✓ Wait for "Hand detected! Make a gesture..."
   ✓ Then perform your gesture
   ✓ Don't rush - hold gesture steady

9 RECOGNIZED GESTURES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Inside the detection zone, you can make these gestures:

1. ✋ HI
   All fingers extended upward, waving motion

2. ✌️ PEACE
   Index and middle finger extended (V sign)

3. ❤️ LOVE YOU
   Thumb, index, and pinky extended (ILY sign)

4. 🙌 THANK YOU
   Open hands

5. 👍 THUMBS UP
   Thumb extended upward

6. 🙏 HELP
   Open palm facing forward

7. 🍽️ FOOD
   Hand pointing to mouth

8. 🥄 EAT
   Eating gesture with hand motion

9. ✊ FIST
   Closed fist
